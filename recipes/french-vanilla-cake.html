French Vanilla Cake (w/ French Vanilla Frosting)

French Vanilla Cake and frosting gives a rich taste that varies from traditional vanilla flavors.

The difference between French vanilla flavor and traditional vanilla flavor resides in the fact that French vanilla is said to resemble the flavor of a rich French vanilla custard. This cake uses similar ingredients to that custard to achieve a true French vanilla flavor without artificial ingredients.


Ingredients:

Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpse flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large egg yolks, at room temp
  • 2 large eggs, at room temp
  • 1 (7-in) vanilla bean
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract
  • 3/4 cup whole milk, at room temp
  • 1/2 cup sour cream, at room temp

Frosting:

  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups sifted powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extrat
  • 1/8 tsp salt
