Pho

Pho is a Vietnamese dish consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat.

Making your own fabulous bowl of Vietnamese pho at home is not hard, all you need is a straightforward recipe, a few secrets and a nice big stockpot. Ingredients

Broth:
  • 5 to 6 lbs of beef knuckles or leg bones
  • 6 quarts cold water
  • 2 medium onions, quartered
  • 4-inch piece of fresh ginger, halved lengthwise
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 1 tblsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tblsp fennel seeds
  • 6 star anise
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 1 black cardamom pod
  • 1 1/2 tblsp salt
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1-inch piece yellow rock sugar
